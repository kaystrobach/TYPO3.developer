Get in touch with the community

The TYPO3 community offers lots of resources to get in touch with other users, developers or people around the globe.
We really want you to participate, this thinking is motivated by our slogan "Inspiring people to share".

To get access to some of the resources you need a TYPO3.org account. These accounts are generally free and you won't get spammed.

Official Communication channels

  • TYPO3.org
  • TYPO3.org forum
  • TYPO3.org mailinglists (connected to the forum)

Official Security and News channels

  • TYPO3 Security Bulletins
  • Development News
  • Community News

Social Communication channels

  • freenode #typo3 and #typo3-cms
  • twitter.com/typo3
  • facebook.com/typo3
  • google plus

Developer Communication Resources

  • Forge - collaborative development plattform
  • Gerrit - code review system
  • Etherpad - work on documents
  • Wiki - share your Knowledge
  • slack, mainly used for internal stuff, request invitation via admin@typo3.org
